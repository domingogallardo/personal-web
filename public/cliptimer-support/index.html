Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • How do I paste tasks?
    Use the keyboard shortcut ⌘V to replace tasks or ⇧⌘V to append tasks.
  • How can I copy a summary of my tasks?
    Press ⌘C to copy the current task summary.
  • Can I pause or restart tasks quickly?
    Yes! Use ⌘P to pause the active task and ⌘R to restart the last paused task.
